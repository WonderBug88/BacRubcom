How it works

The product story is easy to understand in three beats.

The site makes it obvious that Bacrub is about awareness, composure, and better decisions—not novelty for novelty’s sake.

Suggested homepage angle

“Know your level. Keep your cool.”

Step 1

Power on and breathe

Turn it on, use the mouthpiece, and let the sensor do the read. The form factor keeps it low-profile.

Step 2

Read the result instantly

The OLED gives quick BAC feedback and status cues without needing an app or a complicated setup.

Step 3

Take a beat before the next move

The massage end-cap turns the interaction into a pause. That pause is part of the value proposition.

Why this positioning works

Most breathalyzer pages feel utilitarian. This one can feel lifestyle-forward.

That is the opening. You can tell a story around confidence, discretion, and personal rituals—not just test accuracy or hardware specs.
