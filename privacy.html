BacRub Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

BacRub uses this site to share product information and collect early access interest. This policy explains the basic information collected through the current static launch site.

Information we collect

If you submit the early access form, we may collect your email address and any technical details needed to process that request. The current public site does not ask for payment details, health records, government IDs, or blood alcohol concentration readings.

How we use information

We use early access information to send BacRub launch updates, availability notices, product announcements, and related communications.

Sharing

We do not sell early access email addresses. We may use service providers to operate the website, manage email communications, or protect the site.

Contact and updates

This policy may be updated as BacRub moves from early access to launch. Future versions should reflect the actual email, commerce, analytics, and support tools connected to the site.
